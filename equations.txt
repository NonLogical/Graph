Acceleration = DeltaVelocity/DeltaTime
DeltaVelocity = DeltaDistance/DeltaTime
DeltaTime = TimeFinal - TimeInitial
DeltaDistance = DistanceFinal - DistanceInitial
Force = Mass * Acceleration
VelocityFinal^2 = VelocityInitial^2 + 2*Acceleration * DeltaDistance
DistanceFinal = DistanceInitial + VelocityInitial*Time + .5 * Acceleration * Time^2
VelocityFinal = VelocityInitial + Acceleration*DeltaTime
Work = Force*Distance
